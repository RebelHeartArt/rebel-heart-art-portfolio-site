COMMISSIONS

To order a custom work, contact me and we’ll discuss your project, time frame and budget. To pay, I can either make a listing through my shop, or you can pay directly to me through PayPal or Venmo. Prices are the same through either choice.

To book a commission, I require a non-refundable fee of $25 for orders under $100, and $50 for orders over $100. This fee will go towards your total order. Please go to my shop link and pay in the listing under “Booking Fee UNDER $100” OR “Booking Fee OVER $100”. Once paid, I will contact you. If you do not hear from me within 24 hours, please email me at rebelheart1170@gmail.com I will make you a custom listing for the remaining balance, unless you want to pay in full.

I DO NOT begin any work, including drafts, without full payment.

Once I receive payment, I will design a draft for your approval. Once approved, I will begin work. Updates will be sent daily as I’m working on your piece.

There will be a charge of $25 per change after physical work begins.

No refunds for Custom work once work begins.

PAYMENT

As a disabled person with health issues, I understand the need for payment plans. All you have to do is ask! Payment plans are available in 4 monthly installments. I will not remind anyone of payment due. It is solely the client’s responsibility to send payments on time. Add a reminder to your calendar.

I accept only PayPal and Venmo for payments. I can email a PayPal invoice for those who do not have PayPal. You do not need an account to pay. Just a credit/Debit Card.

*I also give 10% Veterans discount! Send proof of Military Service for this discount.

*Postal Money orders are accepted. Work doesn't start until payment is received.

*Checks are accepted. Work doesn't start until the check clears the bank.

REMINDERS FOR PAYMENT(s)

I will not remind folks for payment. Add a reminder to your calendar. A missed payment of more than 14 days will not be refunded in full. I reserve the right to hold a non-refundable deposit from the total as my time is valuable.

COMMUNICATION is KEY. If something comes up, please let me know. I will work with you.

If there's no communication, I have no choice but to remove you from the list forfeiting any payments made.

IN STOCK ITEMS

  • MUST be paid within 24 hours through my shop.
  • No holds. If you want an in-stock item, get it now, once it's gone, it's gone. If you contact me to remake an item, it will be considered a Commission.

It is up to the customer to make sure the shipping address is correct.

Once your item(s) have shipped, I will pm tracking information.

I do not check delivery status. It is solely up to the recipient to check on the status of their package. If it does not arrive on time, contact me ASAP.

REFUNDS of In-Stock Items ONLY

  • Refunds accepted within 3 days of receipt.
  • Must be in the original package, unopened and undamaged.
  • Customer responsible for return shipping.
  • 25% restocking fee.

SHIPPING

All items are shipped Priority through USPS with tracking and insurance.

Once shipped, I will email tracking information.

It is up to the customer to track their package.

Contact me ASAP if your item is not received within 7 days.
